Secopad 36PCS Non-Slip Bathtub Stickers - Safe and Durable Traction

Keep your bathroom safe with trusted non-slip solutions that work hard for you and your family.

Why Choose Secopad?

Secopad's 36 piece non-slip bath stickers are specially designed to prevent slips on wet surfaces. They feature over 1250 tiny protrusions that create a secure grip without causing discomfort to your skin.

Their non-toxic adhesive ensures firm sticking to smooth surfaces like PEVA bathtubs, while making removal easy and residue-free. This makes them a stress-free, long-lasting option for keeping your bath area safe and slip-free.

Versatile and Practical

These non-slip stickers are not limited to just your bathtub. They can be placed on shower tiles, stairs, or even inside elevators to offer extra safety wherever you need it most. Their replaceable design means you can update or reposition them without any hassle.

Each set includes a silicone scraper, making installation faster and smoother. Say goodbye to traditional anti-slip mats and hello to a clean, modern, and effective solution for all wet areas in your home.
